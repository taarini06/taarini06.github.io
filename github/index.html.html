MY PROJECT

I am going to document my project details here

               In Government hospitals , we may find long queues of patients waiting to get their medicines from the pharmacy. Some of the ailing and aged patients will also be standing in queues , which is truly painful to watch.

           When the Doctor checks the patient ,  he / she should enter the prescription in the HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (intranet system ) which is connected to the Pharmacy .

                Once the pharmacy gets the notification , the pharmacist should take the medicine , pack it and keep it ready for delivery . While leaving, the doctor gives a token to the patient (prescription code) .

                      There should be a waiting hall , where all the patients will wait to collect their medicine(where they can sit) . It also should have a DIGITAL DISPLAY BOARD (like in banks, to display token numbers and counter number). Once the medicine pack is ready , their token number should be displayed on the board . When their token number is displayed on the board , the patient should go to the respective counter, show their token number and collect their medicine pack . With more innovation, delivery of medicine can be done by using an AUTOMATIC VENDING MACHINE , where the medicine pack should be put inside the machine with the patient code on the top of it . When the patient scans the HEALTH CARD into the machine , the pack can be delivered from the machine .

 

                             The medicines can also be home delivered through the PRIMARY HEALTH CENTRE staff . Waiting time for the Patients will be reduced.

Benrefits

                       Patients need not wait in long queues for their medicines. I hope this can also increase the job opportunities .

REQUIREMENTS

 *Intranet Based Hospital Management System

 *Waiting Area with Digital Display Boards

*Automatic Vending Machine
